About Me!

I am Bayan Alharbi, a skilled graphic operator and programmer with over 6 years of experience in the field. My passion lies in creating visually stunning designs and building innovative digital products. With a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from King Abdulaziz University and an impressive GPA of 4.65 out of 5, I have a strong foundation in programming languages and software development principles .

Throughout my 6 years of experience as a graphic operator, I have honed my skills and developed an impeccable sense of design aesthetics and attention to detail. I have also gained extensive experience managing websites, from debugging to regular updates and maintenance. Collaboration is a key aspect of my work, and I am a highly collaborative team player who enjoys working with designers, developers, and other stakeholders to bring projects to life.

In every project I undertake, I value innovation, creativity, and continuous improvement. I believe that each project presents an opportunity to push boundaries, learn something new, and create something truly remarkable.

I am excited about the potential opportunity to contribute my skills and experience to your organization. Please feel free to explore my portfolio to see examples of my work and to learn more about my experience.

Thank you for your consideration.
