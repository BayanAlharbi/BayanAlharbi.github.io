Digitalizing Automated the Medical Internship Unit Students Forms

Digitalizing Automated the Medical Internship Unit Students Forms

×

Digitalizing and automating the medical internship unit students’ forms can streamline the administrative process, reduce errors, increase accessibility, enhance security, and promote sustainability.

  • Certification of Eligibility
    • One of the services that have been adapted to be online as a result of COVID-19 pandemic by Medical Internship Unit (MIU) is the eligibility certificate. This certificate must be received by the Saudi Commission for Health Sciences (SCFHS) to allow students to take the eligibility test. This service allows students to request and receive the certificate online as well as it provides MIU to approve and manage the requests.
  • Student Statement Letter
    • This is an automated online service that helps students to have their statement letters for academic admissions. The service saves students time and effort.
  • Temporary Certificate With GPA
    • Temporary Certificate Form is an online form that allows students to request a temporary certificate from the Medical Internship Unit (MIU) online. Through this e-form MIU can manage requests, approve it and send the certificate via email.
  • Outside Rotation Letter for Medical Interns
    • Intern Students need to seek an outside rotation letter that allows them to spend their intern rotation in a hospital of their choice. Our Outside Rotation Letter Form helps both Intern Students and the MIU medical intern unit to make the process by providing the needed letter in a short time.
  • Medical Internship Leave Request
    • During this challenging period due to COVID-19 pandemic, the Medical Internship Unit (MIU) team adapted its approach to provide online services to students. One of these services was medical intern leave request, this project allowed students to request a medical intern leave and the MIU to manage and approve the requests.

Automated and Authorized Absences

Automated and Authorized Absences

×

The Automated and Authorized Absences service provides students with a convenient and efficient way to request absences for either sick leave or business leave. Students can submit their requests through a user-friendly online e-form and receive automatic confirmation of their submission. The platform automatically notifies the academic affairs and student affairs departments, as well as the students' block coordinators, to ensure that all parties are aware of the absence request. The system is designed to ensure that absences are authorized and accounted for, helping to prevent unauthorized absences and ensure that students stay on track with their academic progress. Overall, the Automated and Authorized Absences service simplifies the absence request process, improves communication, and enhances accountability for both students and academic staff.
